# Product

## Register

product

## Users

AI enthusiasts, builders, and evaluators who want to compare model behavior in a fast, entertaining battle format. They arrive with provider keys or local model access, choose two fighters, pick a mode, and watch the app stream a judged multi-round match.

## Product Purpose

AI Battle Arena turns model comparison into a live arcade-style matchup. Success means a user can configure keys, pick fighters, understand readiness, start a battle confidently, and follow the outcome through clear HP, round, and result feedback.

## Brand Personality

Playful, sharp, kinetic. The product should feel like a compact arcade cabinet for AI model duels: lively during fights, but calm and legible while users configure the match.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS landing-page polish, decorative dashboard chrome, and overbearing arcade styling that makes setup controls hard to scan. Avoid relying on emoji glyphs for essential controls because they render inconsistently across environments.

## Design Principles

1. Make the setup path obvious: configure keys, choose fighters, tune the match, then fight.
2. Keep product controls familiar and readable; save dramatic motion and spectacle for the arena.
3. Show readiness and blockers inline so users understand why the fight button is disabled.
4. Preserve the arcade identity through fighter art, HP, color, and moment-specific motion.
5. Design mobile as a first-class play surface, not a squeezed desktop.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Support keyboard focus states, reduced-motion preferences, readable disabled states, and non-emoji labels for important actions and statuses.
